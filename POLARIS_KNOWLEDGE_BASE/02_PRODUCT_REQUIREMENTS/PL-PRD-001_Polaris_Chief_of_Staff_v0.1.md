# PL-PRD-001 — Polaris Chief of Staff

Version: 0.1  
Status: Engineering Started  
Project ID: PCS-001

## Product Vision
Polaris Chief of Staff is a Builder Operating System.

It reduces Builder mental load by organizing responsibilities, waiting items, compliance, cash flow, meetings, and institutional knowledge into one trusted daily briefing.

## Product Promise
When the Builder opens Polaris each morning, they should know:
1. What requires attention today?
2. What is waiting on someone else?
3. What risks or deadlines are approaching?
4. What is the cash flow position?
5. What meetings require preparation?
6. What knowledge or decisions should be preserved?

## Version 0.1 Scope
- Executive Dashboard
- Responsibility Manager
- Waiting On
- Compliance Calendar
- Cash Flow Snapshot
- Builder Journal
- Daily Build Log
