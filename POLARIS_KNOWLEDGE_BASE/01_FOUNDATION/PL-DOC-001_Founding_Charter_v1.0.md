# PL-DOC-001 — Founding Charter

Version: 1.0  
Status: Foundation Approved

## Mission
Polaris Lab researches, engineers, and validates systems that help Builders reduce mental load, strengthen decision-making, preserve institutional knowledge, and build enduring legacies.

## Vision
To become the world's most trusted institution for Builders by transforming experience into systems, systems into knowledge, and knowledge into enduring impact.

## North Star
To help every Builder create an enduring legacy through trusted systems, disciplined thinking, and continuous innovation.

## Five Pillars
1. Builders
2. Systems
3. Trust
4. Continuous Development
5. Legacy
