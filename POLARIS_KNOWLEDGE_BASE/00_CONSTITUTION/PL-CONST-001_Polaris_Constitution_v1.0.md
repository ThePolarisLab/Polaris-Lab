# PL-CONST-001 — Polaris Constitution

Version: 1.0  
Status: Approved Foundation

## Article I — Builders First
Everything Polaris Lab creates exists to strengthen Builders.

## Article II — Truth Before Comfort
Accuracy is more important than convenience.

## Article III — Verification Before Completion
No work is complete until it has been verified.

## Article IV — Communication Completes Work
The outcome of work must be communicated to the person who entrusted it.

## Article V — Every Repeated Responsibility Deserves a System
This is the Polaris Law.

## Article VI — Knowledge Must Become Institutional
Nothing important should depend upon one person's memory.

## Article VII — Improve the System Before Blaming the Person
People make mistakes. Good systems prevent them from happening again.

## Article VIII — Research Before Automation
Never automate a process that has not first been understood.

## Article IX — Build for Generations
Choose decisions that strengthen the institution over the long term.

## Article X — Leave Every Builder Stronger
Every interaction, product, document, and meeting should leave a Builder better equipped than before.
