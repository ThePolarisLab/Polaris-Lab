# PL-PRD-001 — Polaris Chief of Staff

Version: 0.1  
Status: Approved for Engineering

## Purpose

Polaris Chief of Staff exists to reduce Builder mental load by organizing responsibilities, waiting items, compliance deadlines, meetings, cash flow signals, and knowledge into one daily operating dashboard.

## Version 0.1 Scope

- Executive dashboard
- Responsibility model
- Waiting On model
- Builder journal model
- Initial architecture documentation

## Success Criteria

The Founder can open the dashboard and see a clear daily operating view that reduces mental load.
