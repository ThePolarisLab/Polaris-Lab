# Polaris Vision

Polaris is an Executive Operating System powered by Athena.

It helps leaders understand what changed, identify what needs attention, preserve organizational memory, evaluate evidence, coordinate work, and make better decisions.

## North Star

> Build an Executive Operating System that leaders trust with their thinking because it is consistently reliable, explainable, and improves through accountable use.

## Product direction

Polaris will develop through reusable capabilities rather than disconnected features:

- Athena Core for orchestration, context, planning, reasoning, and tool use
- Work Context for evidence collection and entity resolution
- Organizational Memory for decisions, commitments, outcomes, and learning
- Executive Workspace for tasks, meetings, documents, risks, and decisions
- Knowledge Graph for durable relationships across organizational information
- Integrations for secure connection to business systems

## Boundaries

Polaris does not conceal uncertainty, invent evidence, or remove accountable humans from material decisions.
