## Research Notes

- Complexity alone is not equivalent to a code smell; smell detection requires separate rules, context, and false-positive controls.
- Deterministic metrics should remain authoritative inputs for explainable findings.
- LLM-generated explanations may enrich findings later, but must not overwrite deterministic evidence.
- Repository-wide duplicate and coupling analysis may depend on cross-file dependency capabilities and should be phased carefully.
