# POL-FOUND-002 — Builder Principles

**Status:** Official  
**Classification:** Foundation  
**Adopted:** 2026-07-14  
**Owner:** Polaris Foundation  

---

## Every Builder Matters

> **Every Builder matters.**

Recognition is not the measure of a Builder. Contribution is.

Some Builders create organizations known across the world. Others build quietly, support a family, strengthen a community, teach a craft, solve daily problems, or preserve knowledge that would otherwise disappear.

Polaris recognizes the dignity and value of every Builder, regardless of scale, wealth, visibility, title, or public recognition.

A Builder's worth is not determined by how many people know their name.

It is reflected in the responsibility they accept, the value they create, the people they strengthen, and the wisdom they leave behind.

---

## The Foundation Dedication

> **Dedicated to every Builder whose wisdom deserved to outlive them.**

This dedication expresses the permanent purpose of Polaris.

Polaris exists to help preserve hard-earned experience, connect knowledge across time, strengthen judgment, and allow future generations to begin with more understanding than the generations before them.

No Builder should be considered too small for their knowledge to matter.

No meaningful lesson should be lost merely because its teacher was never famous.

---

## Product Implications

Every Polaris capability should:

1. Respect the dignity of every Builder.
2. Preserve valuable experience regardless of organizational scale.
3. Avoid equating success with fame, valuation, or size.
4. Help Builders pass knowledge to future teams and generations.
5. Make institutional memory accessible, traceable, and enduring.

---

## Permanent Test

> **Does this respect the Builder, preserve their wisdom, and help those who follow build further?**

If the answer is no, the work should be reconsidered.
